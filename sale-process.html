Sale Process

Steps in the Sale Process:

  • Draft the Sale Agreement: Clearly outline the sale price, payment schedule, possession date, and any other conditions agreed upon by both parties.
  • Execute the Final Sale Deed: The sale deed must be signed by both the seller and buyer in the presence of two witnesses.
    The deed must be registered at the local sub-registrar’s office; this involves paying stamp duty and registration fees.
  • Payment Processing: Transfer of funds occurs as per the terms laid out in the sale agreement.
  • Handover of Possession: Once the sale deed is registered, hand over the keys, possession, and any relevant documents to the buyer.
  • Mutation of Property: Update the local municipal records to reflect the change in ownership for future property tax and utility billing.
